HUSBANDRY AGENCY

At Aquantis Ship Agency, we recognize the critical importance of husbandry services in ensuring the safe, efficient, and uninterrupted operation of vessels. Aquantis serves as a comprehensive hub capable of meeting nearly all operational requirements of calling vessels.

Our unwavering commitment to safety, timely transits, and efficient port operations is reflected in the delivery of a full spectrum of husbandry services, meticulously tailored to the specific needs of our principals.

Aquantis Ship Agency distinguishes itself through the provision of an extensive range of husbandry services, designed to support the diverse and dynamic demands of vessel operations within the Turkish Straits.

Our services include:
  • Delivery of cash to the Master
  • Customs clearance and delivery of transit spare parts, crew mail, and courier consignments
  • Coordination of stores and supply deliveries
  • Coordination of bunker and lubricating oil supply
  • Fresh water supply
  • Underwater inspections at anchorage
  • Coordination of survey requirements
  • Coordination of repairs and technical services
  • Garbage disposal
  • De-slopping
  • Passenger/Supply boat services
  • Towage services
  • Crew change formalities in all Turkish ports and straits.
  • Crew medical assistance
  • Transportation services
  • Hotel accommodation
  • Verbal declaration(Customs formalities for repairing the ship's spares)
