SHIPYARD AGENCY

As Aquantis Ship Agency, we serve as a trusted partner for shipyard agency services throughout Turkey. Committed to operational excellence, we ensure effective coordination and seamless communication between shipowners, shipyards, and all relevant stakeholders, supporting the successful execution of maritime projects.

Our shipyard agency services include:

  • Dry-docking and repair supervision
  • Repair and dry-docking coordination services
  • Hull cleaning, deck and engine survey support
  • Annual calibration and testing services
  • Ship recycling coordination services
  • Repair, conversion, overhaul, and maintenance support
  • Tank cleaning services
  • Gas-free certification
  • Verbal declaration(Customs formalities for repairing the ship's spares)
